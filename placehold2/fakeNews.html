I Hate Google Fonts

Words cannot express how much this assignment has made me hate Google Fonts. More specifically, I hate the way Google Fonts does icons. I swear, HALF of the time I spent on this assignment was getting those two icons at the top of the page to work. I simply do not understand why I need to order the icons I want to use in a specific way for them to show up properly on the page. I mean seriously, I spent an hour debugging the icons alone. That's ridiculous. Let me show you.

The first code section is how the link tag is implemented now. The second is another version of that same link tag

    
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Material+Symbols+Outlined:opsz,wght,
FILL,GRAD@24,400,0,0&icon_names=chevron_right,home" />
    

    
<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css2?family=Material+Symbols+Outlined:opsz,wght,
FILL,GRAD@24,400,0,0&icon_names=home,chevron_right" />

The first link tag works. The second one doesn't... WTF. It makes no sense to me why the second version would not work, especially because the home icon appears before the chevron_right icon on their website. Either way, you can expect my future assignments to use minimal icons because of this. It was a massive headache.
